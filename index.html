Pulse24 Hospitals Group

Pulse24 Hospitals, Rajeswari Hospitals Group and Ashwin Hospital serve six locations, including Rameswaram Island, Mandapam, Pollachi and nearby communities, with accessible, reliable and patient-focused healthcare.

Dr. K. Ilayaraja

Founder of Pulse24 Hospitals and Rajeswari Hospitals Group

Dr. K. Ilayaraja built this hospital group with a simple vision: to make dependable healthcare available closer to people in rural and semi-urban areas.

Led by Pulse24 Hospitals, the network now spans 6 hospitals, supporting patients through general care, emergency support, diagnostics, surgical care and community-based healthcare services, including Ashwin Hospital in Pollachi.
